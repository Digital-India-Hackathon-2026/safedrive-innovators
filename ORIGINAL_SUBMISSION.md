## Idea
SafeDrive is an IoT-based embedded safety system designed to prevent road accidents caused by drunk driving by detecting alcohol consumption before a vehicle starts. The system uses an alcohol sensor (such as MQ-3) integrated with a microcontroller (Arduino) to analyze the driver’s breath in real time. If the alcohol level exceeds a predefined safety threshold, the system automatically disables the vehicle ignition using a relay mechanism and triggers an immediate alert through a buzzer. In advanced versions, it can also send emergency notifications to family members or authorities using IoT modules.

This solution addresses a major real-world problem where drunk driving leads to thousands of road accidents and fatalities every year. Currently, most systems rely on post-incident enforcement rather than prevention. SafeDrive introduces a proactive approach by stopping unsafe driving before it begins.

The impact of this system is significant as it can drastically reduce road accidents, improve public safety, and promote responsible driving behavior. It is a low-cost, scalable solution that can be integrated into both private and commercial vehicles, making it suitable for widespread adoption in smart cities and transportation systems.
